ABOUT US

STORE BACKGROUND

BLOCKPARTY is a toy store that was established in 1955 in Manhattan, New York, that features vintage toys--especially robots! During WWII, my grandfather, Devin Robbins, was fascinated with the Japanese culture; he was drawn to their toy robots even though they were generally simple and poor quality in comparison to American models. A year after he came back from the war, he opened BLOCKPARTY. Because he found one during his time overseas, he gave it to my dad as a promise that he would come home. Since all the neighborhood kids would get together and play with their figurines, that is what inspired the name.

ABOUT US

Because of the history of BLOCKPARTY, all of our toys have a Japanese/American twist. We are one-of-a-kind, family owned and operated by my father Nick and myself, Mark Robbins. By creating vintage products and collectables, our focus is so that all ages can enjoy them. Make moments last for your children or yourself with these timeless pieces.
